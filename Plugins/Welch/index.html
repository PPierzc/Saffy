Welch

Is one of the methods for calculating the spectral density.

welch_spectrum(self)

Calculates the Welch spectrum. Adds the values to self.spectrum and self.spectrum_freqs.

welch_mean_spectrum(self)

Calculates the Welch spectrum and then takes the mean value across epochs. Adds the values to self.spectrum and self.spectrum_freqs
